HORROR ENVIRONMENT - ABANDONED HOSPITAL v.2
Intallation Guide

Unity version: 2022.3.17

1. DO NOT overwrite any HDRP files with URP package.
2. Move URP_HE_AbandonedHospital.pckg outside of the HE_HDRP_AbandonedHospital directoy.
3. Completely delete HE_HDRP_AbandonedHospital directory.
4. Double click at URP_HE_AbandonedHospital.pckg, import all files.
5. Delete URP_HE_AbandonedHospital.pckg to optimise project size.
6. All assets and example scene will appear in HE_URP_AbandonedHospital directory.
7. Have fun :)
